Frequently Asked Question

What does Q- Seedrs do?

Q- Seedrs operates a fully regulated online platform that allows people to come together and invest in a selection of assets that might otherwise not be available if investing individually. Q- Seedrs investment committee hand pick assets that meet strict criteria in terms of returns for investors while minimizing investment risk. Q- Seedrs also manage the investment and administration of the investment vehicle to make the investment as hassle free as possible.

Our fees

Initial fee

2.5%

Every investment carries a 2.5% initial fee. This contributes to the cost of setting up the asset structures and administration costs of on-boarding investors.

Management fee

5%

Our management fee is 5% of the gross income. This is designed to cover the ongoing costs of managing the investment company and its assets.

Profit share

30%

Our profit is tied directly to yours! When asset is sold at a profit at the end of the agreed investment term, Yielders will receive 35% of the net capital appreciation.
