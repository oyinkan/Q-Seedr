Frequently Asked Question

What does Q- Seedrs do?

Q- Seedrs operates a fully regulated online platform that allows people to come together and invest in a selection of assets that might otherwise not be available if investing individually. Q- Seedrs investment committee hand pick assets that meet strict criteria in terms of returns for investors while minimizing investment risk. Q- Seedrs also manage the investment and administration of the investment vehicle to make the investment as hassle free as possible.

Why invest with Q- Seedrs?

Q- Seedrs offer opportunities that would be difficult to access individually. By utilizing the power of the crowd and advanced technology, Q- Seedrs is able to offer investment opportunities that would have historically only been available to select investors. Our products are selected to offer market beating sales yields providing a steady income to our investors.

What if something goes wrong with Q- Seedrs?

Q- Seedrs have taken every step to ensure our process is well thought out and our investors’ money is in safe hands at all times. Our team of experts conduct extensive market research on each product we make available to the crowd. Each product is vetted against our high standards and we have taken the necessary precautions with regards to insurances on all of our assets. An investors money is held in a ‘Particular Motive Channel’which means that each investment is totally secured, and if the unforeseen does happen, your investment in the PMC remains intact. A director will be appointed in line with terms set out in the shareholders agreement. The director will administer the requirements set out in the shareholders agreement and oversee the repatriation of funds to investors.

What if I’m not a Nigeria citizen? Can I still be a Seedr?

Our aim is to make our investments available to all, however, regulatory restrictions mean that citizens and residents of certain jurisdictions are unable to invest on our platform at present. We encourage all interested investors to register as this would allow us to assess the viability of increasing our geographic reach in the future.

How long is a typical Q- Seedrs investment?

Each investment plan will have a typical investment lifespan of 1-6months. The Q- Seedrs team run various financial models to determine the optimum length for an investment in order to maximize returns for our investors. Investment length and estimated returns are listed on each investment opportunity. Early exit is possible but this could adversely affect the total return on investment.

At the end of the investment, how does the product get sold?

Prior to the end of the investment term, the property is re-valued and listed on the open market for sale. Once a sale is agreed, Seedrs will manage the completion of the sale with solicitors. The funds generated from the sale will be redistributed in accordance with your investment terms.

Can I invest through a Limited Company?

Yes, you can! When you register on our website and go through the sign-up process, please select the tick box stating 'Limited Company' and follow the onboarding verification process.

How often does the investor receive his/her shares of the returns generated from the investment property? Is there an exit strategy?

Returns are made quarterly or monthly depending on the chosen investment. All payments are made directly to the electronic wallet account that is set up by you when you make your first investment. You can then transfer this money to your bank account or let it accumulate to make further investments. Most of our investments have a defined exit and this allows you to maximize your returns. However, as a shareholder you are also free to leave the investment whenever you want by re-listing your products on our platform.
Please note: there may not be sufficient demand to sell your investment on the platform.

What is Agribusiness crowdfunding?

Agribusiness crowdfunding is a process where multiple investors come together to acquire investment in any of / a combination of farming, livestock and grains storage management with a view to getting a share in the accruing returns.

How does Q- Seedrs find the Products it lists?

The Q- Seedrs investment committee have combined Agribusiness investment experience of over 25 years. Their experience ranges from Farming, livestock and grains storage management, acquisition and disposals. They find investment through their network of contacts and through analyzing both on and off-market opportunities.

What’s the process of being a Seedr?

We have tried to make becoming a Seedr as easy as possible. You can join the platform by clicking ‘sign up’ on the top right of the website homepage. Once all the details are filled out correctly, we will provide you with an ID to sign into the platform. You can then start your investment journey.
This information requested when signing up is necessary in order to comply with the Financial Conduct Authority. We need to assess your suitability for investing in product via our platform. This process can be entirely completed online. It is important to note that all of your data is stored securely and is never shared.

Can I invest in more than one product and have a portfolio?

Absolutely! You can invest in as many products as you like provided you have the minimum investment required to access the opportunity.
*Before investing in multiple product, you should obtain independent financial advice to ensure your product portfolio is part of an overall asset allocation suitable to your personal circumstances.
You will also be able to track your investment in each of your assets on our easy-to- use online portfolio tool. This means you have complete overview of your investments from anywhere in the world (provided you have an internet connection).

Are your investments Shariah compliant?

Q- Seedrs PMC’s is an investment platform structured in accordance with Shariah principles. Our platform does not use any banks to fund an investment under any debt based loan which charge interest. We, at Q- Seedrs, acquire products debt free as funded by the crowd, so an investor's money goes straight into the profit accruals.

What is an ‘PMC’?

Q- Seedrs product investments are owned by a ‘Particular Motive Channel’, or PMC. This is the name given to anEnterprise Company set up for a specific purpose. In our case this will be agribusiness investment.

What sort of return can I expect?

The return the investor gets is on each PMC advert under the heading ‘Pricing Method’. Each investment opportunity has its own unique amount of return. You can select performance based on your investment objectives. Investing in livestock generally offers short term returns which provide a regular income as well as capital appreciation which is determined by market forces. We use historic data and forecasts from reputable surveyors and analysts to provide estimated capital appreciation.

How does Seedrs make money out of this?

Seedrs charge each PMC a fee for administration (2.5%), management (5%), and profit share (35%). The administration fee is payable when investing. This is to cover the overheads associated with setting you up as a client. The management fee is a monthly charge on gross rent and is designed to cover the management of the PMC and its assets. The profit share is payable on exit. This has been designed to reward Yielders should the investment offered by us prove to be successful for you.

What if my circumstances change and I need to sell my shares early?

Each investment has a designated time frame in order to increase your potential returns. However, we realize that there will be times when this period does not necessarily meet the investor’s needs such as when circumstances change. In this case, we offer a secondary market where we can help find a buyer on our platform. We also allow the seller to find their own buyer for the shares as well. The transfer of shares incurs a processing fee of 20000.
Please note: there may not be sufficient demand to sell your investment on the platform
