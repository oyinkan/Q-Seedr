Invest in a free-range calf

Invest in a 12 to 18 month old calf that is raised to produce premium free-range beef for a period of 6 months.

We maintain the calf at no extra cost to you until it is ready to sell in six months. Should something happen to your cow we replace it.

Earn a return between 10% and 14% per annum on your investment. You can re-invest in another calf and repeat.

Major details about this program

Mininum Investment Required

The total minimum investment is N600,000 and investment is accepted in three instalments of N200,000 at intervals.

1
Cost of Cow Procurement

The cost of procurement for 3 cows is N300,000 at N100,000 each and The cost of feeding, medication and management for 3 years is expected to be N300,000.

2
Expected Return

In three years, your expected returns is 7- 9 cows, excluding the initial 3 you started with (we expect a mortality rate of 20%).

3
Total Returns & Monitoring

The total returns to investors on a block of 3 cows is 10 - 12, including the initial cows. Q-Seedrs would strive to reduce mortality rate with monitoring from professional veterinarians

4
Initial Cow Sales

After 3 Years, Investors may decide to sell the Initial 3 Cows at about 40% Profit and allow the remaining 7 or 9 cows to continue with the process. We can do about 18 cows in 6 years.

5
Investor Discussion

Q-Seedrs & investor can discuss dynamics after the first 3 years to agree on increasing the number of cows, selling and/or breeding the remaining for meat, and work together to begin milk production

6
Investment Sustainability

The projection of Q-Seedrs Investment scheme is 900 cows in 5 years and, if the need to setup a diary plant arises, the investor and Q-Seedrs will negotiate terms for continuity and sustainability.

7
Agreement with NAPRI ABU ZARIA

Q-Seedrs has an MOU with NAPRI A.B.U Zaria and Real people concept with respect to artificial insemination of hybrid sperms and fertility treatment for high milk yield and high meat yield. More updates as the events unfold.
